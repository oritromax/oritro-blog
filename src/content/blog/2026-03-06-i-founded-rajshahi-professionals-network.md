On a facebook post, i asked if anyone living in Rajshahi, working in the Technology space, wanted to have a Iftar togather. To my absolute surprise, more and more people started to engage with my post, wanting to become a part of a community. So we ended up opening a whatsapp group, inviting everyone to gather up for a iftar party. 

![Group Photo](https://img.sglab.ioritro.com/i/gyxoatUi)

Finally we fixed a date, March 6, 2026. And there then, we officially formed "Rajshahi Professionals Network". 

More details can be found here [RPN Blog](https://rpn.bd/blog/rpn-iftar-mahfil-march-6th-2026)
